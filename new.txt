# Mid-Year Self Evaluation

## Mid-Year Summary

I consistently took end-to-end ownership of initiatives—from opportunity identification and analysis through implementation, stakeholder alignment, monitoring, and continuous improvement. ## Key Contributions & Impact

### Fraud Strategy Innovation & Business Impact

Built a reusable fraud strategy development framework across MBNA and TDCT, including a **20+ feature library** and a **decision-tree-based Python framework** to identify missed fraud opportunities and accelerate rule development.

This reduced analysis turnaround from **days to a few hours** and led to deployment of **4+ fraud rules generating CAD 500K+ monthly fraud mitigation impact**.

---

### Fraud Strategy Optimization & Operational Efficiency

Conducted rule and queue performance analysis across MBNA, reorganized queue structures based on FPR performance, decommissioned underperforming rules, and expanded auto-block decisioning for high-confidence fraud signals.

This created approximately **80 alerts/day of additional operational capacity**, improved automation, and strengthened fraud decision efficiency while monitoring unblock rates to balance customer impact.

---

### VIP Customer Experience Initiative

Partnered with Fraud Operations and Technology teams to improve the fraud review experience for VIP customers by designing a prioritization framework, implementing interim operational solutions ahead of platform releases, and building monitoring for weekly performance tracking.

This improved customer experience while maintaining fraud risk controls.

---

### Reporting, Monitoring & Portfolio Visibility

Redesigned the weekly dashboard reporting process by optimizing SQL/Python workflows across Citadel and DSAP, reducing KPI preparation time from **3 hours to under 40 minutes**.

Also took ownership of weekly dashboard reporting and presenting updates to the CPM audience.

Built portfolio monitoring frameworks and reusable data mappings across MBNA and TDCT, reducing analysis time by approximately **90%** and enabling faster access to application, alert, fraud, and operational KPI insights.

---

### Proactive Fraud Risk Management

Actively contributed to monitoring emerging fraud trends and ongoing fraud attacks, supporting rapid analysis, mitigation strategy development, and implementation of fraud controls to reduce portfolio exposure.

Also monitored downstream impacts such as unblock rates to assess strategy effectiveness and customer impact.

---

### Process Improvement & Team Enablement

Improved team efficiency through automation of recurring BAU reporting using Python, reducing manual effort and improving consistency.

Strengthened knowledge transfer and operational scalability through documentation of FIQM workflows, deployment SOPs, BAU processes, and dependency/request management procedures.
